1961

The Marvelettes

In 1961,"Please, Mr. Postman" was released under Tamla Records. Written by Marvelette Gladys Horton as an adaptation of a blues song, this track was most notable as the first Motown song to reach the number 1 position on the Billboard Hot 100 Pop singles chart. The single achieved this position in late 1961; it hit number one on the R&B chart as well. "Please Mr. Postman" became a number-one hit again in early 1975 when The Carpenters' cover of the song reached the top position of the Billboard Hot 100.
