1960

Smokey Robinson & The Miracles

In 1960,Tamla Records released "Shop Around", which would become the label's first number 1 hit on the Billboard magazine R&B singles chart. Suddenly, what may have seemed like an overconfident dream in Gordy's 'Hitsville' sign began to become a reality. "Shop Around" would be the first No. 1 hit of many to come. It also hit No. 2 on the Hot 100, reached No. 1 on the Cash Box magazine Top 100 pop chart, and is also noted for being the first million-selling record for the Miracles and for Motown Records, as well as a 2006 Grammy Hall of Fame inductee.
