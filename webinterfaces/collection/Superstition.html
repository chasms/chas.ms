1972

Stevie Wonder

In 1972,"Superstition" was released under Tamla Records. It was written, produced, arranged, and performed by Stevie Wonder, when he was 22 years old. It was the lead single for Wonder's Talking Book album, and released internationally. It reached number one in the USA, and number one on the soul singles chart. Overseas, it peaked at number eleven in the UK during February 1973. In November 2004, Rolling Stone ranked the song at #74 on their list of the 500 Greatest Songs of All Time.
